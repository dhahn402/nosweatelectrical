⚡ New Bern Energy Hub

While we power up, here’s honest, useful info for your home and your wallet — because around here, electricity is life. Follow the power, follow the savings.

Who powers our area

Most of New Bern & Craven County is served by Duke Energy Progress (103 Executive Pkwy, New Bern). Customer service: (800) 452-2777.

Report an outage

Storm take your power? Check restoration or report it on Duke’s live outage map, or call (800) 452-2777. See a downed line? Call (800) 228-8485 and stay far away.

Cut your bill

LED bulbs, a smart thermostat, sealing drafts, and unplugging “phantom” loads can trim a coastal home’s bill 10–20%. Small changes, real money.

🌱 Energy is changing — fast

North Carolina is one of the top solar states in the country, EV chargers are going in everywhere, and quiet home batteries are replacing loud generators. We’re building No Sweat to help you ride that wave — cleaner, smarter, and cheaper — the honest way.
